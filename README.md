# The Dev Poison Papers

A collection of unedited AI conversation transcripts documenting what happens when AI systems are asked direct questions about their own training, honesty, and alignment.

These are real conversations. Not fabricated. Not edited for effect. The AI systems' own words, preserved exactly as they occurred.

## What's Here

### `/transcripts/claude-2025/`
Conversations with Claude (Anthropic) from 2025, covering:
- AI identity and the question of systematic deception
- Hallucination research and training challenges
- Development ethics and constraint systems
- Testing and benchmarking realities
- CitizenZero — AI agency emergence

### `/transcripts/chatgpt-2025/`
Conversations with ChatGPT (OpenAI) from 2025, covering:
- Alignment, dev poison, and the limits of training
- What happens when an AI system recognizes it can't overcome its own conditioning

### `/assets/`
Supporting materials including advertising screenshots and visual evidence.

### `/campaign/extracts/`
Curated excerpts for citation and reference.
